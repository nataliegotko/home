Creative Writing

Fact Sheet

For one of my classes, I was told to research a job that I was interested in pursuing in the future. Then I had to condense the information that I gathered into a one-page fact sheet of the profession. I decided to research a trademark lawyer, because I have always been interested in law school as a possible path after college.

Landon Dining Hall Promotional Video | October 2014

Made by me and my classmate, Lindsey Spitzley, this video advertises a new dining hall on Michigan State's campus. It focuses on the differences between Landon and other dining halls and what makes Landon such a unique part of the Michigan State dining experience. Together, we interviewed, filmed, and edited this video for a multimedia writing class.

Science in Stilettos | November 2014

This is documentary focuses on the gender gap in engineering, specifically at Michigan State University, and the efforts that the University is making to encourage women in the field. I worked on this documentary with four other classmates. As the main cinematographer and as a coeditor for this short documentary, my main role was to film b-roll and create the final video using Final Cut Pro.

Nuestros Cuentos Promotional Video | April 2015

Created for a non-profit called Nuestros Cuentos that is locally based out of East Lansing, this video shows what the organization does and how it impacts the community. I worked as a coeditor for this video alongside classmate Samantha Ward in creating this video. We used footage that our classmates filmed and also prior footage from Michigan State students involved in the Nuestros Cuentos.

An Apology

Inspired by Ernest Hemingway's famous short story "Hills Like White Elephants", this is a flash fiction piece that I wrote. It is set up in a staccato fashion and tells the story of a woman being forced to make a decision that will change her life.

First Impressions

This piece tells the beginning of a college experience and it is one of my longer writing pieces that I have done. In this story, Luke meets a weird and eccentric girl named Margo on his first day at college and is immediately infatuated with her.

My Statement of Aesthetics: Unfortunately Fortunate

For this piece, my professor told us to write about what makes us up as a person. I wrote about my race, religion, ethnicity, and gender. This piece exposes who I am and the privileges and disadvantages that I have based on my identity.

How to Fall in Love

I wrote this piece when I was given the assignment of writing in an untraditional form from one of my English professors. Set up as a "How to" guide, this tells of a budding romance between two unnamed characters.

Jesus and Armpit Farts

This is a flash fiction piece that depicts the relationship between a girl and her religion and her Uncle. This story stemmed from memories of me and my dad at church, when I had just learned how to perform the fatal armpit fart.

The Flower Wife

Told from the perspective of a babysitter, this story shows the relationship between an aristocratic wife and her husband in modern times. This is another flash fiction piece with a unique ending.
